Privacy Policy

SnapCanvas Pro - Chrome Extension

Last Updated: January 28, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to SnapCanvas Pro, a browser extension designed to help you capture, crop, resize, and convert images directly within your browser. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data and protect your privacy.

SnapCanvas Pro is built with a Privacy-First philosophy. We believe your data belongs to you, and we are committed to transparency in how our extension operates.

Key Principle: SnapCanvas Pro operates entirely locally on your device. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any of your personal information or images with external servers.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

SnapCanvas Pro is designed to respect your privacy completely. We explicitly DO NOT collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, or any other personal identifiers.
  • Browsing History: We do not track, monitor, or record the websites you visit.
  • Captured Images: Images you capture, crop, or edit remain exclusively on your device and are never uploaded to servers or third-party services.
  • Usage Analytics: We do not use analytics tools, tracking pixels, or any form of user behavior monitoring.
  • Cookies or Tracking Technologies: We do not use cookies, web beacons, or similar tracking technologies.
  • Device Information: No information about your device, OS, or browser configuration is collected.
  • IP Address: We do not collect or log your IP address.

3. How SnapCanvas Pro Works

All functionality operates entirely locally within your browser:

  • Image Capture: Screenshots are processed using browser APIs (Chrome Tabs API) locally.
  • Image Processing: Editing operations (crop, resize, rotate, flip, convert) happen in your browser using HTML5 Canvas technology. No data leaves your device.
  • Image Detection: Our engine scans the current webpage's DOM locally to find images.
  • Local Storage: Preferences such as theme settings are stored in your browser's local storage and never leave your device.

4. Permissions Explained

SnapCanvas Pro requests the following permissions, used only for their stated purpose when you actively use a feature:

4.1 activeTab

Purpose: Captures screenshots of the currently visible area of your active browser tab.

Usage: Triggered only when you click "Capture Visible Area" or similar buttons. Only the active tab is accessed.

4.2 scripting

Purpose: Allows scripts to be injected for advanced features like full-page capture and image detection.

  • Full-page capture: Scrolls and stitches multiple screenshots.
  • Image discovery: Scans the page's DOM for all images, including srcset and background images.
  • Context menu extraction: Extracts images from the current page.

Important: Scripts run only when you actively use a feature and never automatically in the background.

4.3 contextMenus

Purpose: Adds options to your right-click menu.

Usage: Menu items like "Capture Visible Area," "Capture Full Page," and "Edit Image in SnapCanvas Pro" give quick access to features.

4.4 storage

Purpose: Saves your preferences locally.

  • Preferred theme (light or dark mode)
  • UI preferences
  • Temporary context menu data (cleared immediately after use)

Important: Storage is local only and never synced to external servers.

5. Third-Party Services

SnapCanvas Pro does not use any third-party services for analytics, advertising, data collection, or image processing.

5.1 External Resources

The extension uses only local system fonts. External content may appear in:

  • Image Previews: Images displayed via image detection come from the original webpage sources, not our servers.

We have no control over external websites’ privacy practices. Those sites’ policies apply when interacting with their content.

6. Data Retention

  • Captured Images: Stored temporarily while you work; deleted after download or closing the extension.
  • User Preferences: Persist in local storage until browser data is cleared or extension uninstalled.
  • Context Menu Captures: Temporary storage cleared immediately after use.

7. User Rights and Control

  • Access: All data is local and viewable via browser developer tools.
  • Deletion: Clear browser cache/cookies or uninstall the extension.
  • Portability: Images remain under your control and can be saved locally.
  • Opt-Out: No data is collected, so no opt-out is needed. Simply avoid features you are uncomfortable using.

8. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

SnapCanvas Pro does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. No personal data is collected from any user.

Parental supervision is recommended for young users when interacting with content from websites.

9. International Users and GDPR Compliance

  • Right to Access: All local data can be accessed on your device.
  • Right to Deletion: Delete extension data by uninstalling.
  • Right to Portability: Captured images can be saved anywhere on your device.
  • Right to Object: No personal data is processed, so nothing to object to.

10. Security

  • Local Processing: All operations occur entirely within your browser.
  • No Server Communication: Core functionality does not contact servers, except for external webpage images.
  • Minimal Permissions: Only permissions necessary for features are requested.
  • Chrome Web Store Review: Extension meets all store security requirements.
  • Limited External Dependencies: Few libraries to reduce security risks.

While we follow best practices, no software can be guaranteed 100% secure. Keep your browser and OS updated.
